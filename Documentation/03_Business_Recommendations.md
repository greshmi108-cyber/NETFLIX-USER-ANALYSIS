# 25 Strategic Recommendations for Netflix Leadership
### Based on Growth, Engagement, Retention & Revenue Analysis

## Acquisition & Marketing
1. **Reallocate spend toward low-CAC, high-LTV channels.** Campaign ROI analysis shows
   organic and referral users convert cheaper and retain longer — increase referral
   program incentives before scaling paid social further.
2. **Cut or renegotiate the bottom-quartile campaigns by CAC**, identified in the
   Marketing Dashboard, and reinvest that budget into the top 3 ROI campaigns.
3. **Introduce a structured referral program** (e.g., one free month per successful
   referral) — referral users show materially better funnel conversion.
4. **Test regional pricing/campaigns in underpenetrated high-growth countries**
   (per country-level signup and completion-rate data) rather than uniform global spend.
5. **Shift free-trial length experiments** for channels with weak trial→paid conversion,
   since this is the single biggest funnel leak in the acquisition pipeline.

## Retention & Churn
6. **Launch a 90-day onboarding intervention program** — cohort analysis shows the
   steepest drop-off happens in month 1; a guided onboarding flow (curated first-week
   recommendations) can blunt this cliff.
7. **Build a proactive at-risk alert** using recency + completion-rate decline as
   leading indicators, and trigger win-back offers before cancellation, not after.
8. **Address "Price too high" as the #1 churn reason** with a mid-tier ad-supported
   plan or loyalty discount for tenure milestones (12/24 months).
9. **Fix payment failures proactively** — retry logic + card-update reminders reduce
   involuntary churn, which is a "silent" and avoidable revenue leak.
10. **Launch a structured win-back campaign** targeting the "Dormant" segment with a
    limited-time discount and personalized "what's new since you left" content reel.
11. **Reduce churn on the Mobile/Basic plan specifically** — it shows the highest
    lapse rate; investigate whether perceived value (ads, resolution caps) is the driver.
12. **Introduce plan-pause instead of cancel** as a default cancellation flow option to
    convert full churn into temporary pauses, preserving reactivation potential.

## Engagement & Product
13. **Invest in the recommendation engine for new users specifically** — completion
    rate and binge rate are the strongest engagement-to-revenue correlates; a stronger
    "first 7 days" recommendation model compounds into retention.
14. **Increase binge-friendly content formats** (multi-season series, cliffhanger
    structuring) — binge sessions show materially higher retention and LTV.
15. **Prioritize Smart TV and Mobile UX improvements** — these devices show the
    longest average session durations and are the primary engagement surface.
16. **Improve in-app search and discovery** — search-to-watch conversion has room to
    improve; better autocomplete and "trending searches" surfacing can lift this.
17. **Use the Content Engagement Score (not raw view count) for content renewal
    decisions** — some high-view titles have poor completion/rating, indicating
    algorithm-driven but unsatisfying watches.
18. **Double down on top-performing genres per region** rather than a one-size-fits-all
    global content slate — genre popularity is highly region-dependent.
19. **Test autoplay-next-episode more aggressively for at-risk (low-frequency) users**
    specifically, since it's a proven binge/retention lever.

## Monetization & Revenue
20. **Build a structured upgrade nudge campaign** (Basic → Standard → Premium) targeted
    at high-engagement, low-plan users — the highest-LTV conversion opportunity
    identified in the upgrade-path funnel analysis.
21. **Introduce annual billing incentives** (small discount for annual vs monthly) to
    lock in longer-tenure, lower-churn-risk revenue.
22. **Bundle Premium plan with exclusive early-access content** to justify price
    premium and reduce "price too high" churn objections at the top tier.
23. **Monitor ARPU by country and adjust local pricing tiers** rather than a single
    global price ladder, to balance affordability and revenue per market.

## Organizational / Process
24. **Establish a shared weekly Growth/Product KPI review** using the Executive
    Dashboard as the single source of truth, replacing fragmented spreadsheet reporting.
25. **Set explicit LTV:CAC and Monthly Churn Rate targets per quarter** and review
    marketing and content investment decisions against them, not against vanity
    metrics like raw signups or raw view counts.
